How to Play

  • Player X always goes first
  • Players take turns placing their mark in empty squares
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins
  • When all 9 squares are full, the game is over (a draw if no winner)
  • Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs
  • If the cards match, they will remain face up
  • If they don't match, they will flip back over
  • Complete the game by matching all pairs in the fewest moves and shortest time
